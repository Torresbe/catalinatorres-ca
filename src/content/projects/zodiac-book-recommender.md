## The work

The first attempt at putting two worlds in the same room: a decade of editorial work, and the digital humanities tooling I had just walked into during the master's. The choice of subject was unserious on purpose — a book recommender that matched readers to fiction through their zodiac sign. The choice of method was the entire point.

## What it was for

To play. To stop being intimidated by the code. To find out from the inside how digital tools are built, so I could understand what programming — and later, AI — would let editorial work do that hadn't been possible before. The aim was never to become an engineer. It was to be a humanist who could read what the tools were saying back, and decide what was worth doing with them.

## Try it

A reduced version of this project runs on the site. Pick a sign, get a recommendation. The logic is a small version of the original — characters and arcs matched against what each sign tends to recognize.
